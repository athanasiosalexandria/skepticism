About Me

I am currently an undergraduate sophomore at the University of California, San Diego, where I am pursuing a B.A. in Classical Studies with a minor in Philosophy. I currently have senior standing and expect to graduate in the Spring or Summer of 2026. Upon graduating, I plan to pursue a JD-PhD Program and further my study of the Classics. As part of my studies, I will be conducting research on the interaction of Hellenistic Philosophy and early Christian asceticism through the Honors program of the UC San Diego Classics Department.

As a student of the Classics, my interests include Latin, Ancient Greek, Hellenistic Philosophy, Early Christianity, Classical and Hellenistic Greek Antiquity, and the Roman Empire.

I was born in Torrance, California. I spent part of my childhood in Greece before my family moved to Palos Verdes, California. I attended Palos Verdes High School, where I graduated as a member of National Honor Society, National English Honor Society, Spanish National Honor Society, California Scholarship Federation, and Tri-M Music Honor Society. I have lived in San Diego since 2023, when I moved to continue my studies at UC San Diego.

I was raised bilingual and am fluent in English and Modern Greek, with limited proficiency in Latin and Spanish. I also have elementary reading proficiency in Ancient Greek.
